Air Writing Recognition using CNN

The system will include a secure login and registration page to authenticate and manage user accounts, ensuring a personalized and secure experience. The aim is to provide an intuitive interface for users to write in the air, have their gestures recognized, translated into Hindi.

Auto Night Street Light System

The Smart Street Light System helps reduce energy waste and high electricity bills by using LED lights instead of sodium lamps and adding IR sensors for automatic control. This system turns the lights on only when needed, preventing unnecessary energy use caused by manual switching. It is currently used for one-way highway traffic and operates sensors even during the day, but the lights do not turn on before sunset. This setup lowers light pollution, reduces CO₂ emissions, cuts maintenance costs, and improves lighting efficiency, making it a smart solution for saving energy in the future.
